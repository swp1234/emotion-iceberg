About This Test

Understanding the Emotion Iceberg Model

The emotion iceberg model illustrates how most of our emotional experience lies beneath the surface. Just like an iceberg, what others see is only a fraction of what we truly feel. This test examines 10 real-life scenarios to reveal the gap between your surface reactions and your hidden emotional depths.

Why Emotional Awareness Matters

Understanding the gap between your surface emotions and deep feelings is key to emotional intelligence. By recognizing patterns in how you mask or suppress emotions, you can build healthier relationships, reduce stress, and develop greater self-compassion. Your iceberg type is the first step toward emotional growth.
